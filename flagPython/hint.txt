In the shadowy halls of Malfoy Manor, a chilling conversation unfolds in the presence of the Dark Lord.
The Death Eaters are tense, knowing their master's wrath and the implications of their plans being discovered. 
Some of the Parselmouths present understand the seriousness of the situation whereas others are confused.

The conversation is overheard by Draco Malfoy and he scrambles the it within an enchanted scroll for future translation.
Draco also has a faulty translator that he stole from the room earlier. Will it be useful?

The secret is present in another manuscript for which the key is present in the conversation.
